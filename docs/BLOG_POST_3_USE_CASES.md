## Use Case 1: The Crypto Investor

### Meet Alex

**Profile**:
- Age: 35
- Occupation: Software Engineer
- Assets: $500,000 in cryptocurrency
- Family: Wife and 2 young children
- Location: San Francisco

### The Problem

Alex started investing in Bitcoin in 2015. Over the years, his portfolio grew to include:
- 10 BTC (Bitcoin)
- 150 ETH (Ethereum)
- Various altcoins and DeFi positions
- NFT collection worth $50,000
- Staked assets generating passive income

**His concerns**:
1. "If something happens to me, my family won't know how to access my crypto"
2. "I can't just write down my seed phrases - too risky"
3. "My wife isn't tech-savvy enough to navigate crypto wallets"
4. "Traditional estate planning doesn't understand cryptocurrency"
5. "I need something that works globally - we travel a lot"

### The Traditional Approach (Doesn't Work)

**Option 1: Write it down**
- ❌ Seed phrases on paper can be stolen
- ❌ House fire destroys everything
- ❌ Wife might accidentally throw it away
- ❌ Kids could find it and "help daddy"

**Option 2: Tell his wife**
- ❌ She might forget the complex process
- ❌ Information could be outdated when needed
- ❌ Creates security risk while he's alive
- ❌ She's not comfortable with crypto

**Option 3: Use a lawyer**
- ❌ Lawyers don't understand crypto
- ❌ Seed phrases in legal documents = security risk
- ❌ Probate takes months (crypto markets move fast)
- ❌ Costs thousands of dollars

### The Solution: Decentralized Digital Will

**What Alex does**:

```
Step 1: Create Encrypted Package
- Exports all wallet seed phrases
- Includes instructions for each wallet
- Adds exchange account details
- Records video explaining everything
- Encrypts it all with AES-256

Step 2: Set Up Beneficiaries
- Wife: Primary beneficiary (100%)
- Brother: Backup (if wife unavailable)
- Kids: Trust fund (when they turn 18)

Step 3: Configure Heartbeat
- Interval: 30 days
- Grace period: 14 days
- Method: Biometric + wallet signature

Step 4: Distribute Key Shares
- Share 1: Smart contract
- Share 2: His hardware wallet
- Share 3: Wife's encrypted email
- Share 4: Brother's encrypted email
- Share 5: DAO oracle
```

**What happens if Alex dies**:

```
Day 0: Alex stops recording heartbeats
Day 30: System notices missed heartbeat
Day 44: Grace period ends
Day 45: Smart contract releases Share #1
Day 46: Wife receives notification
Day 47: Wife collects shares from:
  - Smart contract (automatic)
  - Alex's hardware wallet (she has it)
  - Her encrypted email (she has password)
Day 48: System reconstructs key
Day 49: Wife downloads encrypted package
Day 50: Package decrypts automatically
Day 51: Wife watches Alex's video
Day 52: Wife follows instructions
Day 53: Family has access to all crypto
```

**Total time**: 53 days (vs 6-12 months with traditional will)

### The Impact

**Financial**:
- ✅ $500,000 in crypto preserved
- ✅ No probate fees ($15,000 saved)
- ✅ No lawyer fees ($5,000 saved)
- ✅ Passive income continues flowing

**Emotional**:
- ✅ Wife has clear instructions
- ✅ Alex's video provides comfort
- ✅ No stress about "figuring it out"
- ✅ Kids' future secured

**Practical**:
- ✅ Works globally (they travel)
- ✅ No paperwork or courts
- ✅ Automatic and fast
- ✅ Private and secure
