🔄 DATA FLOW:

User clicks category → Modal opens with template
                              ↓
                    User fills form fields
                              ↓
                    Validation (required fields)
                              ↓
            Generate structured data (plain text)
                              ↓
                Encrypt with AES-256-GCM
                              ↓
            Split key (Shamir 5 shares, 3 threshold)
                              ↓
                Upload file to IPFS (optional)
                              ↓
                Save to IndexedDB
                              ↓
            Distribute shares to beneficiaries
                              ↓
                    Success! ✅
